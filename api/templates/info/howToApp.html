How To: Android App

1. Go to the App Download tab to download the .apk file for the app.

2. Download the .apk and install it on your Android device. You may need to edit your Android phone permissions in order to install the application.

3. Open the app. You will be greeted by a login screen.

4. To create an account, tap on "Create one here"

5. Fill out the username and password field, and tap "Create Account"

6. This will take you back to the login screen. Please login with the username and password you just created. This will take you to the dashboard where you can see all events.

7. To search for an event, tap the search bar and type in your search query. Hit return. This will take you to a page with the results from your search.

8. To join an event, tap on the event and you will be taken to a detailed view of that event where you can join it. Tap the "Join Event" button to join the event. You will see your name pop up in the Users list.

9. To leave the event, simply tap the red "Leave Event" button

10. To see events you have joined, go back to the events screen and tap on the menu button. This will open up a menu. Tap on "My Events"

11. To create an event, go back to the dashboard and tap the "+" symbol. This will take you to a "Create Event" screen where you can enter in your event information. All fields are required. Tap "Create" to create it.

11. To log out, go back to the dashboard. Tap on the menu icon. Tap log out. This will sign you out and take you back to the login screen
