How To: Website

1. Go to this link to see the website. You will be greeted by a homepage where you can log in or create an account.

2. Click on create an account.

3. Enter in a username and password.

4. You will be sent back to the homepage. Please log in using the same username and password you just created.

5. Your username should pop up next to a logout button in the top right corner. You will be sent to the events page where you can see all listed events. You will notice 4 tabs in the navigation bar, Home, Info, Events, and My Events. You can only access Home, Info, and Events when signed out.

6. To join an event, click on it. You will be taken to a detailed event page.

7. To join the selected event, click Join Event. Your name will pop up in the Users list, and the blue Join Event button will be replaced by a red Leave Event button.

8. To leave the event, simply click Leave Event. Your name will be removed from the list

9. To see all the events you have joined, click My Events. It will show all the events you have joined and whether they are past or upcoming.

10. To create an event, go to the events page and click the green Create Event button.

11. A popup will appear where you can enter in all the information for your event. All fields are required. Hit Create to create it.

12. You will be redirected to the events page, where you can see and join your newly created event.

13. Click the log out button at any time in the top right corner to log out.
